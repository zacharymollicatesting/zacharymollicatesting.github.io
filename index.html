Advanced Fabrication,
Experimental Architecture.

Headquartered at the Architectural Association's Hooke Park campus, student of the Design + Make programme explore achitectural design at the point of physical production. Supported by a diverse team of expert practitioners, fabrication and design methodologies are developed at the intersection of craft knowledge, innovative technologies and natural materials. Through the realisation of full-scale prototypes and permanent buildings, students conceive and construct experimental architecture driven by the principle of design-through-making.

About Design + Make

The AA’s satellite Hooke Park campus is Design + Make's laboratory for architectural research through 1:1 fabrication.
