MILL COVE PARK

On St Margaret’s Bay

Mill Cove Park is a 65 unit community of townhomes and duplexes situated in a park-like setting on St Margaret’s Bay five minutes from Hubbards. Mill Cove Park has mature trees, common lawns and quiet streets with no through traffic.

Why live in Mill Cove Park?

Family Accommodations

Mill Cove Park offers light-filled 3 and 4 bedroom accommodations with private yards and parking.

It’s All New Again

Under new ownership and management since 2016, a major renewal program began including landscaping and full renovation of all units.

A Community School is Adjacent

As a residential community, Mill Cove Park also sits adjacent to the Aspotogan Consolidated Elementary School -- a community school which serves as a community centre offering a variety of programs for all ages. Children can walk to school in 5 minutes without crossing any road.

Close to Shopping and Halifax

  • 5 minutes to full service shopping and medical services in Hubbards
  • 15 minutes to Chester
  • 20 minutes to regional shopping in Tantallon at Exit 5
  • 40 minutes to Halifax

Local Recreation and Community Services

  • Two public beaches within walking distance
  • Daycares and nursery schools in Hubbards
  • Several churches nearby
  • Hubbards Sailing Club (sailing classes for kids)
  • Aspotogan Rdige Golf Course 1 minute away
  • J D Shatford Memorial Public Library, Hubbards
  • St Margaret’s Bay Trails
  • Shatford Trust – student scholarships
  • Aspotogan Heritage Trust - community programs
