Join us June 7 – 9 in celebrating Lunenburg’s birthday!

yellow: #fbaf33 blue: #66c6bd grey #404041 red #cb3627

Once again this year, various groups, businesses and organizations are hosting events and offering specials and discounts to help mark the Town's 266th birthday. Local residents and visitors alike are encouraged to take some time to enjoy all that our beautiful town has to offer. The actual birthday is June 7 but most activities will take place the following weekend.

Restaurants and retail shops will have specials on over the weekend – look for 'Be a Tourist' posters. A map of locations for some of the weekend's activities is available here. Connect with us on Twitter and Facebook.

Lunenburg Art Map
Launch & Tour – Open Studios, Demonstrations, Exhibitions & Receptions

Various Locations

10:00AM - 6:00PM

The Lunenburg Art Map is launching it's 2019 curated guide to visual art and fine craft throughout the Town Of Lunenburg. The 2019 Lunenburg Art Map boasts 25 artists and venues including professional working artist's studios and galleries. Each venue on the map will be open to the public and offering exhibition opening receptions, refreshments or demonstrations. The Lunenburg Art Map will guide you to all of these galleries and studios. This is a great opportunity to visit the studios that are usually open by appointment and meet Lunenburg's visual arts community. The Lunenburg Art Map was founded in 2005 as part of the Art Gallery Association Of Lunenburg. The mandate of AGAL has been to promote and establish a community of professional artists and galleries in Lunenburg.
