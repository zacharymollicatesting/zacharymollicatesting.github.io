The Design + Make programme is now accepting applications for the September 2020 intake to its MArch and MSc courses. Prospective students and applicants are strongly encouraged to visit Hooke Park. For any inquiries regarding the programme or to arrange a visit, please contact us. For questions regarding specific application procedures please contact the Graduate Admissions office or refer to the AA's application guide.

Apply Now

MArch Requirements: professional architecture degree (5 years generally; 4 years from certain countries) or “equivalent through experience” for exceptional cases.

MSc Requirements: architecture, engineering or other relevant discipline including art and design where appropriate interest/experience has been demonstrated.

Validation: The Architectural Association is approved by The Open University as an appropriate organisation to offer higher education programmes leading to Open University validated awards.
