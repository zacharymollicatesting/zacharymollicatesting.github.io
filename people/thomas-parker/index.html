Thomas Parker
Technical Consultant

Thomas Parker is an architectural designer and filmmaker who studied at the Bartlett School of Architecture. His interests lie in experimental and speculative forms of spatial design, explored through the deployment optical devices and scenarios for the generation of unique architectural conditions. His work explores various forms of analogue and digital technology, including 3D scanning, with a focus on the way we might translate between different modes of representation to generate new architectural languages.
