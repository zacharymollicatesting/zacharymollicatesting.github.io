Where are we?
Hooke Park is located about 3 miles south-east of the town of Beaminster, close to the village of Hooke. The surrounding woodland is open to the public. While there is no public access to the buildings, visitors are welcome by prior arrangement.

Reach us by car
Road access is via the main entrance gate on the north-east side of the site, close to Hooke village. Approach from Crewkerne via the A303 and A3066, from Beaminster via the B3163, or from Dorchester via the A37 and A356. The approach road to Hooke Park is narrow and single-track in places.

Reach us by train
The most convenient route for travel from London is to Crewkerne (2.5 hours). Dorchester, Yeovil and Maiden Newton stations are also near by. See Trainline for details of train departure times. Taxis for transfer to Hooke Park can be pre-booked with Craig’s Taxis (01308 861000).
