July 28 - August 2, 2019

The Best of Boxwood 2019

Musique Royale presents The Best of Boxwood 2019- combining colorful and eclectic sounds with the finesse of classical technique creating a salute to the traditional dance music of the Canadian Maritimes, along with that of Scotland & Ireland!

For more please visit: https://www.boxwood.org/canada_lunenburg

Featuring

Chris Norman (flute/pipes/vocals), Eamon O'Leary (vocals/guitar/banjo), Alexis Chartrand (fiddle/violin), Bronwyn Thies-Thompson (vocals), Amy O'Neill (concertina/flute)
