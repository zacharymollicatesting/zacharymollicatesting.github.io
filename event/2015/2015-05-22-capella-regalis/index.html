May 15 - 22, 2015

Capella Regalis – 2015 Spring Tour in five communities

The final concert on Friday May 22 will be preceded by a free Open Rehearsal and Pre-Concert Talk, from 5:30 – 6:15 pm. All members of the public are welcome, including families with boys ages 6 – 12 who are interested in learning more about the choir.

Featuring

Capella Regalis (choir)

Performances

St. John's Anglican Church
Friday 15 May – 7:30 PM

Tickets are $20 and are available at Shop on the Corner in Lunenburg and by reservation from the MR Box Office at (902) 634-9994 or musiqueroyale1985@gmail.com and at the door.

St. Mark's Anglican Church
Saturday 16 May – 7:00 PM

Tickets are $20 and are available at Petite Riviere General Store

7095 Highway 331, Broad Cove

393 St. George Street, Annapolis Royal, NS

First Baptist Church
Friday 22 May – 7:00 PM

Tickets are $20 (free for all elementary school children) and are available at King’s Co-op Bookstore (902.422.1270 ext. 261), on Eventbrite at www.capellaregalis.eventbrite.ca, and at the door.

1300 Oxford St, Halifax
