In celebration of its 35th anniversary, Musique Royale presents a virtual edition of its Summer Festival, with new concerts featuring early music and more, filmed in historic venues across Nova Scotia, to be enjoyed wherever you are. Concerts are released each Saturday at 7:30pm from August through September, and stay available for viewing on YouTube and Facebook for two weeks.
